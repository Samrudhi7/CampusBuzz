About CampusBuzz

CampusBuzz was created to bring college life together in one place. From the excitement of fests to the energy of workshops and clubs, we help students stay connected, informed, and part of everything happening on campus. No more missing events — CampusBuzz makes participation seamless and fun.

Our Mission

We aim to make campus life more engaging and accessible for every student. By centralizing events, notifications, and registration into a single platform, we ensure that students spend less time searching and more time experiencing.

Why CampusBuzz?

Stay Connected

Never miss an opportunity to join events, clubs, or workshops. CampusBuzz keeps you in the loop with timely updates.

Save Time

All events, registrations, and reminders in one place — no more back-and-forth emails or confusion.

Experience More

From casual hangouts to major fests, discover and participate in all the exciting happenings on campus.

Our Vision

We envision a campus where students are fully engaged, informed, and empowered to make the most of their college years. CampusBuzz is not just an app — it’s a community that celebrates student life.
